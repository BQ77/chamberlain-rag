myQ Secure View 3-in-1 Smart Lock - Product Overview

Source: Chamberlain Group press release, January 6, 2026

Chamberlain Group (CG), a Blackstone portfolio company and global leader in intelligent access and monitoring solutions, unveiled the myQ Secure View 3-in-1 Smart Lock on January 6, 2026. This breakthrough device represents CG's strategic evolution from single-category leadership to a complete smart home security ecosystem, bringing decades of access control expertise from the garage to the front door.

What it is:
The myQ Secure View 3-in-1 Smart Lock combines three devices in one: a premium smart lock, a 2K HDR video doorbell, and a biometric access system in one intelligent device. It provides five ways to enter your home: face access, fingerprint recognition, PIN, mobile app, or key.

Why it is significant:
By integrating a camera into a smart lock, the product enables the fastest unlock in the market - just two seconds. The integrated camera also gives homeowners crystal-clear visibility to every arrival at their front door. The myQ Secure View is described as the most advanced video lock ever, setting a new standard for front-door access and security.

Strategic context:
This is Chamberlain Group's boldest expansion from garage door innovation to intelligent access for the entire home. The product anchors CG's broader ecosystem vision where AI provides intelligence and devices act in harmony across all home access points, giving users total control through one app. The expansion connects the front door experience with the garage access control that has made the myQ platform trusted by more than 14 million people.

CEO statement:
"Our customers have trusted myQ to secure their garages for years, and now we're bringing that same reliability and intelligence to the front door with one simple, powerful device that provides everything you need to keep your home's entry points secure. This isn't just about physical security; it's about intelligent access that feels effortless and adapts to the way people actually live." - Jeff Meredith, Chamberlain Group CEO
