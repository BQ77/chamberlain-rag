myQ Secure View 3-in-1 Smart Lock - Entry Methods and Key Features

Five entry methods:
The Secure View provides five ways to enter your home:
1. Face Access - facial recognition for hands-free entry
2. Fingerprint recognition - biometric authentication via fingerprint scanner
3. PIN entry - typed PIN code for keypad access
4. Mobile app control - remote unlock through the myQ app
5. Traditional key access - physical key as a backup

Note on biometric availability:
Face and fingerprint access are not available in Illinois, Texas, or outside the U.S. and Canada due to local regulations.

Key product features:
- Industry-leading two-second unlock speed with the dependability and ease myQ customers trust
- 2K HDR video doorbell with color night vision providing crystal-clear visibility of every arrival at your front door
- AI-powered smart detection identifies people, vehicles, animals, packages, and motion with intelligent alerts
- Seamless myQ integration connecting front door access with garage door control and other myQ devices including the best-selling Smart Garage Video Keypad
- Engineered to perform in extreme conditions from -4°F to 140°F
- Rechargeable lithium battery lasting up to 6 months per charge
- Zero downtime: a fully charged spare battery (sold separately) is always ready to go
- Pairs with the myQ Chime for clear, in-home alerts even when phones are on silent
- Easy Bluetooth setup with multiple tones, perfect for homes without wired chimes

Built for real life:
Understanding that no two homes or homeowners are the same, CG designed the myQ Secure View with flexibility and reliability. Whether homeowners prefer Face Access when their hands are full, fingerprint access for family members, temporary PIN codes for service providers, or remote app control for guests, the system adapts to real-world scenarios with the everyday ease myQ customers already trust. Easy installation requires only common household tools, and the battery is designed for quick removal and charging.
